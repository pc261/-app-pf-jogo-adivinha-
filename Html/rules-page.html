Game Rules

Welcome to the NumberGuess game! Here are the rules:

  • Guess a number between 1 and 100.
  • You have 10 attempts to guess the correct number.
  • After each guess, you will receive feedback on whether your guess was too high or too low.
  • If you guess the correct number, you win!
  • If you run out of attempts, the game is over.

Good luck and have fun!!!
